oṃ namaḥ paraśambhave

śaśadharasadṛśasaroje alake devākṣayānake caraṇau sitarajabhāsvaravimalau śambhor nityaṃ [ ] pāntu janān 1

yasya prasāram atulam ābrahmastambabhuvanaparāntagam sa śrīnāthakuleśaḥ śrīkubjikādhidaivo jayati 2

MNTRku/MNTRkāraḥ pārthivo bījo aMNTRbji/MNTRkoparisaṃsthitaḥ MNTRkā/MNTR kālī śivagā vakrā sā śrīkubjikā jayati 3

sṛṣṭā yayā parā vic ca haṃsavarttidvīpakujā sā janeśī kalau kālī saṃsthitā paścime gṛhe 4

vedāt tu paramaṃ śaivaṃ śaivād dakṣiṇam uttamam dakṣiṇāt paścimaṃ śreṣṭham ataḥ parataraṃ na hi 5

vijñāya śrīkubjikāyāḥ śrīkujeśvarasahitāyāḥ nṛṇāṃ svargāptihetvarthaṃ nityāhnikam ihocyate 6

jñānārṇavam agādho ’yaṃ śrīśambhuśāsano mahān tatra pāraṃ paraṃ gacchan vīcī śrīkaṇṭhasaṃjñakaḥ 7

taccaraṇair yathā dṛṣṭaṃ śrīmatpaścimaśāsane pāramparyakramāyātaṃ sugopyam anulikhyate 8

svagotrāṇāṃ hitārthāya śrīmacchrīkaṇṭhasūnunā muktakena kiyadgūḍhaṃ sopadeśaṃ jayena tu 9

ādhārādisusūkṣmāntaṃ parāntaṃ paranirṇayam kathitaṃ yena suspaṣṭaṃ taṃ śrīkaṇṭhaguruṃ name 10
śrībhairava uvāca

athātaḥ sampravakṣyāmi āśramāṇāṃ sthitiṃ priye yenāsau vartito yogī melakaṃ trividhaṃ labhet 1

gṛhasthaḥ sālayī caiva cāravī ca vanāśrayī naiṣṭhikaś caiva yogī ca ṣaṭprakārāśramasthitiḥ 2

sādhikāraḥ sapatnīkaḥ śāstrajñas tattvavedakaḥ kṣamī ca guptaliṅgī ca gṛhasthaḥ samayasthitaḥ 3

kṛṣivāṇijyakartā ca sevako ’patir eva vā yena kenaiva puṇyena ācāryas tena pūjyate 4

ācāryeṇa kriyā kāryā samyak siddhā kulānvaye niraṃśasya mahādevi kuladravyais tu sammatā 5

āgataṃ na tyajed yas tu gatvā naiva parigrahet na kevalaṃ manuṣyāṇāṃ sa gurur devadurlabhaḥ 6

ekāntasthaḥ sapatnīkaḥ suguptaś ca prakāśanaḥ saṃtyaktānugrahaḥ śāntaḥ sālayī khecaraṃ vrajet 7

niṣkalatraḥ sadaikākī niḥsaṅgaḥ samayasthitaḥ pīṭhādigrāmaparyantaṃ paryaṭec carukāśayā 8

viprādiśvapacānteṣu sarvabhakṣaḥ kṛtāntavat cāravyo vyāpakā devyaḥ sarvavarṇeṣu saṃsthitaḥ 9

labdhadīkṣo ’bhiṣiktaś ca mantrasiddhiṃ samīhavān guruvaktravrate niṣṭhaḥ saṃsthito girigahvare 10

kṣetrābaddho jitāhāraḥ kandamūlāśanasthitaḥ jitendriyaḥ sadotsāhī vanacārī ca sādhakaḥ 11

samīhate na bhuktiṃ yaḥ siddhayo bahudhāś ca yāḥ lokātītaś ca saṃnyāsī muktim evābhivāñchati niḥsaṅgo guptaliṅgī ca paraś cādvaitanaiṣṭhikaḥ 12

nānārūpadharo nityaṃ jaratkanthāvaguṇṭhitaḥ nirdvandvo nirbhayaḥ śānto niścaryāyogatatparaḥ 13

saṃdhyāyāṃ paryaṭed bhaikṣyaṃ sadaiva carukāśayā nirjane paramānandasaṃsthito ’harniśaṃ dṛḍhaḥ siddhayogānvayī yogī yogasiddhiṃ samīhavān 14

asiddhe yogavijñāne krīḍāṃ kurvanti ye narāḥ patitāḥ paśavas te ’tra kulācāraviḍambakāḥ 15

āśramāṇāṃ bahirbhūtāś caryāṃ kurvanti lolupāḥ bhakṣyante kuladevībhiḥ pūtanābhir upadrutāḥ 16

dīkṣābhiṣekasampannaḥ śambhoś caraṇacandragaḥ gurukramaparo bhūtvā nityaṃ naimittikaṃ bhajet 17

mantro vidyā parā mudrā yoginī kuladevatā etāḥ pañcavidhāḥ śaktir liṅgayonyāṃ vyavasthitāḥ 18

tasmān na dūṣayel liṅgaṃ raupyahemādibāṇakam ṣaḍaśrapīṭhikāyuktaṃ pūjyaṃ siddhiphalapradam atrodare jagat sarvaṃ kramaś cāpi mahākramaḥ 19
prātar{ }utthāya|

ūkāraṃ vāyubījaṃ tadupari varuṇaṃ vajrapāṇiṃ tadūrdhve kālaṃ varṇāntayuktaṃ tadupari paramaṃ vahnibījaṃ sahaṃsam induṃ binduṃ layāntaṃ sitakamalaparaṃ kṣīradhārā sravantaṃ dṛṣṭvā kūṭaṃ tu nityaṃ dahati kulamalaṃ merutulyaṃ hi pāpam 1
iti kūṭaṃ svānte ’rkarāśisadṛśaṃ pāpanāśāya dhyāyet|

jalasnānaṃ prakurvīta pañcāṅgam athavā punaḥ jalasnānam āplavanam āmūrdhniparyantaṃ bhajet 2
kṣoṇījalatejavāyvīśa parābījāni binduyuktāni| jale saptadhā vinyasya śambhumantram{ }uccaran tridhā snāyāt|| athavā pañcāṅgaṃ pādau hastau mukhaṃ prakṣālya| astrarahitamantrasaṃhitayā sitarajaḥkumbhamudrādiyogena vauṣaḍantena mantrasnānam|| jalāśayād{ }utthāya| āsanāṇunā kaṭisūtram| māyābījena kaupīnam| navātmanā yajñopavītam| radanākṣarayā mudrikām| madanena yogapaṭṭam| ākāśenottarīyam| vāyubījena prāvaraṇam| gṛhasthasya śuklapaṭam| āsanenāsanam| yatigṛhasthayor{ }mṛgavyāghrājinam| navātmanā veṇudaṇḍam{ }iti|| yajñopavītalakṣaṇaṃ vakṣye|*

sthūlaṃ caivātisūkṣmaṃ ca tyaktvā sūtram anuttamam kartitaṃ kulajābhiś ca triguṇaṃ triguṇīkṛtam 1

vartayitvā prayatnena kārayet trisaraṃ punaḥ eṣa sthānadvayaṃ kṛtvā sāvitryā granthayet tataḥ 2

dhautaṃ śuklaṃ śubhaṃ sūtram alakṣmīmalanāśanam yasya vidhṛtamātreṇa pumān brahmasamo bhavet 3

triguṇaṃ triguṇīkṛtya veṇīṃ kṛtvā prayatnataḥ tenaiva ca śatārdhābhir uttarīyā tv aghakṣayā 4
iti sottarīyayajñopavītalakṣaṇam||

bho sādhakendrāḥ śṛṇutaikavācyaṃ sarvānvayoktaṃ phalasiddhihetoḥ tāvan na siddhiṃ pralabhanti santo yāvan na mantrāḥ praṇavādiśeṣāḥ 1

pūrvaṃ yā sūcitā śaktiḥ kujākhyā krūrasaṃyutā ā ity ādhārabhūtā sā prasūtisthitisaṃhṛteḥ 2

ambikā bhujagārūḍhā guhyaśaktyā trimaṇḍalaiḥ māyā devī puṣṭikarī vāribhūtā tu sā smṛtā 3

nāgasthā kusumā devī guhyayātha trimaṇḍalaiḥ śrīkarī tejabhūtā tu tṛtīyā śakti raudriṇī 4

dīpanīśikhiparāmbājhaṭendrabindu viśvahā vilomenoddhṛtā devī khecaratvapradāyikā 5

anugrahyārcitaṃ haṃsaṃ prāṇināṃ hṛdi saṃsthitam uddhṛtam ākāśabhūtaṃ sudhānirvāṇadāyakam 6

kṣmācakram uktaṃ caturasrapītaṃ khaṇḍenduśubhraṃ varavāricakram tejolakaṃ raktaguṇāradivyaṃ vātākhyaṣaṭkoṇacakraṃ sudhūmram 7

svacchārkatulyaṃ viyadādhibimbaṃ bhūtāni pañcāny uditāni śāstre eṣāṃ maheśaṃ sitaśāntarūpaṃ cāturbhujaṃ padmasite niviṣṭam 8

dakṣeṇa hastena dadhāti śūlaṃ dānaṃ krameṇaiva ca tathāpareṇa kārddhābhayaṃ vāmakare vidhatte pañcātmamantraiḥ karadehavaktrān 9

netrāsraśūnyaṃ karakāyavaktraṃ japan krameṇaiva dharādimantraiḥ lakṣair vivṛddhyai tithilakṣamātraṃ ante daśāṃśaṃ havanaṃ ca dhīraiḥ 10

icchāpradaḥ śrīpraṇaveśvaro ’yaṃ bhaktārtihartā guṇalokadātā surair munīndrair manudaityasiddhaiḥ prāptaṃ padaṃ yājanahāvanānte 11
iti pañcapraṇavoddhāraḥ
