Editorial

  • Editorial declaration:
    • normalization:
      • None. The difference between ẏ and y, ḷ and l, ṛ and ḍ, and between capital and lower-case vowels needs to be removed to obtain more normal Sanskrit spelling. Numerous (all?) cases of intervocalic ch need to be changed to cch. Distinction between b and v needs to be introduced.
